Overview

CampNeja is a full-property takeover of a 100+ acre forest retreat: massive lodge, bonfires, volleyball, pickleball, kayaks, trails, and game rooms — plus a 16-person NYC van full of chaos barreling into Pennsylvania. It’s technically a birthday thing (two weeks after my actual birthday), but not a classic birthday party. It’s a crossover weekend for all the different friend groups in my life — low-stress, high-vibes, and guaranteed stories.
