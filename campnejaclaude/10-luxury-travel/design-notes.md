# Luxury Travel Design Concept

## Visual Direction
**Theme:** Aman/Soho House quiet luxury with linen textures
**Mood:** Serene, sophisticated, understated, refined
**Reference:** Luxury hotel websites, Aman Resorts, Soho House, The Line

## Color Strategy
Warm neutrals (sand, beige, taupe). Soft sage and terracotta accents. Ivory and linen backgrounds. Natural, earthy sophistication.

## Layout
- Generous whitespace
- Clean, minimal layouts
- Large, beautiful photography
- Soft, natural color blocks
- Breathing room everywhere

## Components
- **Buttons:** Subtle, minimal, refined borders
- **Cards:** Soft shadows, linen backgrounds
- **Navigation:** Minimal, disappears on scroll
- **Typography:** Generous leading, understated

## Effects
- Subtle linen/fabric textures
- Soft shadows (never harsh)
- Gentle fade animations
- Natural, organic movement
- Quiet, refined interactions
