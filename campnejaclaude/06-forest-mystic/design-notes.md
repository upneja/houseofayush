# Mystical Forest Cult Design Concept

## Visual Direction
**Theme:** Bioluminescence, ancient runes, fog, enchanted dark forest
**Mood:** Mystical, otherworldly, ethereal, ancient magic
**Reference:** Fantasy forests, Avatar bioluminescence, Nordic runes, witchcraft aesthetics

## Color Strategy
Deep forest blacks and dark greens as base. Bright bioluminescent greens, teals, blues as glowing accents. Fog grays for atmosphere.

## Layout
- Dark, immersive backgrounds
- Glowing accent elements
- Mystical fog overlays
- Floating, ethereal components
- Rune symbols as section markers

## Components
- **Buttons:** Glowing green with pulsing animation
- **Cards:** Dark with bioluminescent borders
- **Navigation:** Translucent with glow effects
- **Runes:** Custom mystical symbols throughout

## Effects
- Glow/bloom effects
- Particle systems (fireflies, magic dust)
- Fog animations
- Pulsing bioluminescent accents
- Parallax floating elements
