# Cinematic Universe Design Concept

## Visual Direction
**Theme:** Dune/Bond epic with dramatic lighting and metallic accents
**Mood:** Epic, mysterious, sophisticated, larger-than-life
**Reference:** Movie posters, Dune aesthetic, James Bond title sequences

## Color Strategy
Desert tones (sand #C9A86A, copper #B87333) meet midnight blue (#0D1B2A). Metallic accents. High contrast dramatic blacks.

## Layout
- Widescreen aspect ratios (16:9 sections)
- Cinematic full-bleed images
- Dramatic overlays and gradients
- Floating text on dark backgrounds
- Epic scale typography

## Components
- **Buttons:** Metallic gradient, bold typography, glowing hover
- **Cards:** Dark with subtle metallic borders, cinematic images
- **Navigation:** Sleek top bar, fades in/out on scroll
- **Overlays:** Dark gradients over all photos

## Photography
- Dramatic lighting and contrast
- Desaturated with golden hour tones
- Heavy vignettes
- Widescreen crop ratios

## Animation
- Slow, dramatic fades (1-2s)
- Parallax scrolling
- Glow effects on metallic elements
- Epic reveal animations
