# Typography System: Secret Society

## Typeface Strategy
Ceremonial elegance meets occult mysticism with serif authority and geometric precision.

## Primary Typefaces

### Display / Headers
**Cormorant Garamond** (or Playfair Display)
- Weights: 300, 400, 500, 600, 700
- Usage: H1, H2, event name, ceremonial headings
- Characteristics: Classical serif with refined elegance, perfect for mystical authority

### Body / UI
**Crimson Text** (or Spectral)
- Weights: 400, 400i, 600, 700
- Usage: Body copy, descriptions, schedule details
- Characteristics: Readable serif that maintains ceremonial feel without sacrificing legibility

### Accent / Labels
**Cinzel** (for special elements)
- Weights: 400, 600, 700
- Usage: CTA buttons, section labels, sigil captions
- Characteristics: Imperial Roman capitals for commanding presence

### Monospace / Details
**JetBrains Mono**
- Weight: 400, 500
- Usage: Dates, prices, technical info
- Characteristics: Modern contrast to classical serif system

## Type Scale

```
H1 (Hero Title):     72px / 4.5rem    | Cormorant | 600 | -0.02em
H2 (Section):        48px / 3rem      | Cormorant | 500 | -0.01em
H3 (Subsection):     36px / 2.25rem   | Cormorant | 500 | 0em
H4 (Card Title):     28px / 1.75rem   | Cormorant | 600 | 0em
Body Large:          20px / 1.25rem   | Crimson   | 400 | 0em
Body:                18px / 1.125rem  | Crimson   | 400 | 0.01em
Body Small:          16px / 1rem      | Crimson   | 400 | 0.01em
Label/CTA:           14px / 0.875rem  | Cinzel    | 600 | 0.05em (uppercase)
Caption:             14px / 0.875rem  | Crimson   | 400 | 0em
Detail:              12px / 0.75rem   | JetBrains | 400 | 0em
```

## Line Heights
- Display: 1.1
- Headings: 1.3
- Body: 1.7
- Labels: 1.2

## Special Treatments

### Ceremonial Dividers
- En dash (–) wrapped in ceremonial gold
- Sigil bullets for lists
- Gold underlines with fade effect

### Drop Caps
- First letter of major sections: 3-4x body size
- Ceremonial gold color
- Cormorant Garamond 700

### Number Treatment
- Pricing: Tabular numerals, Cinzel 600
- Dates: JetBrains Mono 500, ceremonial gold accent

## Responsive Adjustments
- Mobile: Reduce display sizes by 30-40%
- Maintain contrast between display and body
- Keep Cinzel uppercase labels at 12-14px minimum
