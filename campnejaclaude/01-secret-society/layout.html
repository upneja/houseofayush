The Gathering

CampNeja is a full-property takeover of a 100+ acre forest retreat: massive lodge, bonfires, volleyball, pickleball, kayaks, trails, and game rooms — plus a 16-person NYC van full of chaos barreling into Pennsylvania.

It's technically a birthday thing (two weeks after my actual birthday), but not a classic birthday party. It's a crossover weekend for all the different friend groups in my life — low-stress, high-vibes, and guaranteed stories.

Join us for three days of forest adventures, late-night conversations, competitive games, and the kind of memories that become legendary.

The Ritual

Friday — Arrival & Settling

Arrivals throughout the day. Claim your bed (RSVP order priority). Explore the property, get oriented, find your favorite hammock spot. Dinner at the pavilion as the group assembles. Bonfire under the stars. Late-night games for the nocturnal crew.

Saturday — Maximum Chaos

The main event. Volleyball tournaments, pickleball battles, kayak expeditions, paddle boat races, trail exploration, game room domination, hammock philosophy, strategic naps, wandering, doing absolutely nothing if that's your vibe. Big dinner as the sun sets. Night chaos of undetermined nature.

Sunday — Slow Departure

Leisurely breakfast. Share stories from the night before. Pack up slowly. Return to civilization, forever changed.

All schedules are suggestions. The forest decides.
