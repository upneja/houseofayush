# High Fashion Editorial Design Concept

## Visual Direction
**Theme:** Dior/Celine minimalism with B/W editorial grid precision
**Mood:** Sophisticated, austere, luxurious, refined
**Reference:** Vogue spreads, fashion lookbooks, luxury brand campaigns

---

## Color Philosophy
Pure black (#000000) and white (#FFFFFF) dominate. Minimal gray scale for depth. Rare accent: forest green or muted gold (used sparingly, <5% of design).

**Contrast:** Maximum. Sharp edges. No gradients except for photo overlays.

---

## Layout & Grid
- **12-column grid:** Strict adherence
- **Asymmetric layouts:** 60/40, 70/30 splits
- **Generous whitespace:** 100-200px margins
- **Editorial blocks:** Full-bleed images alternating with text blocks
- **Magazine-style:** Content presented like fashion editorial

---

## Iconography
- **Minimal to none:** Rely on typography
- **When needed:** 1px hairline icons
- **Style:** Ultra-simple geometric shapes
- **No decorative elements**

---

## Textures & Backgrounds
- **Flat colors:** No textures
- **Photography:** High-contrast B/W conversion
- **Paper white backgrounds**
- **Deep black sections** for contrast
- **Grain:** Optional subtle film grain on photos only (2-3%)

---

## Components

### Buttons
- **Primary:** Black fill, white text, 1px border
- **Secondary:** White fill, black text, black border
- **Hover:** Invert colors
- **Shape:** Perfect rectangles, sharp corners
- **Padding:** Generous (20px × 60px)

### Cards
- **Borderless or 1px hairline**
- **Pure white or black background**
- **High-resolution images**
- **Minimal text overlay**

### Navigation
- **Fixed top, transparent → white on scroll**
- **Horizontal, uppercase, 11px Futura**
- **Extreme letter-spacing (0.2em)**
- **Underline on active (1px)**

---

## Photography Treatment
- Convert to B/W (or keep minimal color)
- High contrast (+20%)
- Grain filter (subtle)
- Full-bleed or inset with generous margins
- Never use borders

---

## Logo/Wordmark
```
CAMPNEJA
MAY 1—3
```
- Bodoni 400, stacked
- Extreme simplicity
- Optional: Minimal geometric mark (single line, triangle, or dot)

---

## Animation
- **Fade-in only:** No slides, no rotations
- **Smooth, slow transitions:** 0.8-1.2s
- **Parallax:** Subtle on hero only (0.5x speed)
- **Delays:** Strategic 0.2-0.4s staggers

---

## Responsive
- Maintain grid integrity
- Stack asymmetric layouts vertically
- Preserve whitespace ratios
- Font sizes scale proportionally (not abruptly)
