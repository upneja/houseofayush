# Bauhaus Modernist Design Concept

## Visual Direction
**Theme:** Geometric shapes, primary colors, clean modernist grids
**Mood:** Bold, rational, constructivist, timeless
**Reference:** Bauhaus movement, De Stijl, constructivist posters

## Color Strategy
Primary colors (red #E63946, blue #1D3557, yellow #F1C453) as bold accents. Black, white, gray as foundation. High contrast color blocking.

## Layout
- Perfect grid system (12-column)
- Geometric shapes as decorative elements (circles, squares, triangles)
- Asymmetric balance
- Color-blocked sections
- Swiss-style composition

## Components
- **Buttons:** Bold color fills, uppercase Futura, perfect rectangles
- **Cards:** Color-blocked borders, geometric accents
- **Navigation:** Horizontal bar with geometric dividers
- **Shapes:** Circles, triangles as visual punctuation

## Animation
- Linear movements (no easing curves)
- Color transitions
- Geometric shape reveals
- Grid-based animations

## Typography
- Futura for all headings (Bauhaus classic)
- High contrast sizing
- Perfect alignment
- Uppercase labels
- Tabular spacing for numbers
